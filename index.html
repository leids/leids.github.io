CACP LEIDS

Canadian Law Enforcement Information Data Standards

LEIDS is a group focused on driving interoperable information sharing between Law Enforcement and partners within Canada. We are guided and report under the Canadian Association of Chiefs of Police.

Vision

Enable common law enforcement data sharing, regardless of system or technology

Mission

Develop and promote interoperable data sharing satandards to increase sharing and reduce costs.

  • Define common data exchange requirements for police agencies and partners
  • Either adopt existing data exchanges or create new exchanges. All LEIDS exchanges are NIEM (National Information Exchange Model) based.
  • Manage a set of criteria to ensure “LEIDS Compliance” for agencies or vendors.
  • Support adoption of interoperable data exchanges for law enforcement and the justice community as a whole.
